/ About

I build fast. I build right. I build things that ship on day one.

I'm a full-stack developer specializing in modern React/Next.js applications with real-time backends. Over the last five years I've shipped products for early-stage startups, SaaS companies, and agency clients — everything from two-sided marketplaces to internal analytics platforms.

My focus is on clean architecture, accessible UI, and production discipline: proper auth, role-based access, testing, observability, and deploys that don't break at 2am.

I work best as an embedded contractor or short-term partner — brought in to spike a feature, de-risk a migration, or turn a designer's Figma into a live product in weeks, not months.

Every project below is a self-contained working demo. Click, click, poke around — that's what showcases are for.
